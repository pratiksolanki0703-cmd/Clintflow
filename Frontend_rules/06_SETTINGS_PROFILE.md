# PURPOSE

The Settings section allows freelancers to manage their account, business profile, preferences, AI usage, billing, and security.

Everything should be easy to find.

No hidden settings.

No complicated menus.

---

# SETTINGS NAVIGATION

Settings contains:

- My Profile
- Business Profile
- Branding
- Notifications
- Email Settings
- AI Credits
- Billing & Plans
- Security
- Preferences
- Connected Apps (Future)
- Danger Zone

Always use a left sidebar on Desktop.

Use tabs on Mobile.
